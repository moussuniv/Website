Über uns

Having our roots in the Hanseatic merchant city of Bremen easily explains why we feel so at home at sea and in the marketplace. Founded in 1880, our family-owned company soon cast away from its local waters to launch its services and spare parts on the international market.

Today, we supply shipbuilders, ship owners, shipping companies, the marine industry and heavy industry with everything they need to get engines and ships moving – whatever their size and type.
